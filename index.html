Bio

Currently undertaking a PhD in Electroacoustic Composition at the University of Manchester, Harry's research focuses on data sonification of dynamic optimisation algorithms and developing machine learning methods for generative compositional methods. Harry is also an alumni of the University of Leeds with a BSc in Mathematics and Music, and MMus in Critical and Experimental Composition.
